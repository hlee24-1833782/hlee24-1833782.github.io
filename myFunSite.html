Indecisive? Worry less by scrolling through our delicious options of places to eat and satisfy all your cravings!

Chung Chun Rice Dog

Popular Korean street food made with rice flour batter wrapped in hot dogs, spicy sausages, or mozzarella hot dogs. With so many options to choose from, this place will be sure to satisfy all your cravings!

Icon

Din Tai Fung

Craving some dim sum? Check out Din Tai Fung: famous for not only their dim sum but also their green beans! There are locations in Bellevue and Seattle, so find the one nearest to you!

Icon

The Pink Door

Who doesn't love a good plate of pasta? Known for its delicious noodles and ambience, this restaurant is the place to go if you are feeling a little more fancy.

Icon

Beecher's Handmade Cheese

Looking for a quick bite to eat? Originated in Seattle, this is the place to go for some warm mac and cheese. The creamy yet filling meal will have you wanting to go back!

Icon

The Dolar Shop

The Dolar Shop is a famous hot-pot restaurant with variety of ingredients and fresh food ready to serve every customer. There is reason to this fame!

Icon

Taco's Chukis

Finding authentic and delicious tacos are proving to be more difficult. However, Taco's Chukis has it all! Authenticity, flavor, and good food. It is a must have!

Owners of the Restaurants

Understanding where the food comes from and the inspiration of the business makes the entire experience of eating more heartwarming. Read more about some of the business owners below!

Not having any expectations for the success of the business, the owner created this local taco restaurant out of wanting to share his love for the culture and the food. The secret hidden spot has attracted more customers out of curiosity and now has grown into a successful chain.

- Roberto Salmerón CEO - Taco's Chukis

Passionate about serving authentic, full-flavored foods, Kurt established this business to demonstrate how quality ingredients can provide delicious meals.

- Kurt Beecher Dammeier CEO - Beecher's Handmade Cheese

Jackie offers an escape from the conventional menu through the warm ambience of the restaurant. The mysterious yet amazing food expereince through quality ingredients always brings customers back.

- Jackie Roberts CEO - The Pink Door
